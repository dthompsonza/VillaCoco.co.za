Villa Coco Guesthouse

Situation

Villa Coco Guesthouse is centrally located in Durban North, minutes from the trendy Broadway area. It has good access to all highways, making this location ideal for businessmen and tourists alike.

click images to enlarge

Supermarkets, shops and restaurants are minutes away, and with Durban North nestled between Durban Central and Umhlanga, other venues such as Wilson Wharf in Durban Harbour or Gateway on Umhlanga Ridge are within easy striking distance.
For swimming, Suncoast beach is 10 mins away and for fishing, Virginia is just down the hill.

Accommodation

Various Options

The main unit has two rooms (Coco Rouge and Coco Vert) , which can be rented separately or as a double room in the case of a family.
The second unit is a separate, single room cottage (Coco Rouge).
Secure off street parking is available for three cars and all the rooms have separate entrances.

click images to enlarge

Coco Rouge

A tastefully furnished room with massive Bali bed, TV and bar fridge.
The outside deck allows for privacy and enjoying a sundowner with sea views.
There is a common area with gas barbeque should guests want to take it easy at home rather than dine out. A modern open plan bathroom with shower and separate bath complete the picture.

Coco Vert

A slightly smaller room with two single beds, ideal for the cost conscious business person, or for the kids if taken in conjunction with Coco der Mer. It has its own shower and toilet.

Coco de Mer 

A lovely room, with stunning four-poster bed. Coco de Mer also has its own deck with seaviews. TV, modern open plan bathroom and access to the communal barbeque area complete this package.
