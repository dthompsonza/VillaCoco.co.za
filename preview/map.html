Note: The physical address is 43 Kelvin Place, Durban North - however, access to the guesthouse is off High Ridge Road which runs parallel to Kelvin.

Getting there:

When approaching Durban North look for the Riverside Hotel (overlooking the Umgeni River) or alternatively the KFC (on Northway, opposite the Riverside Hotel). At the traffic lights just past the KFC, turn into Tyne Place and head west (ie, away from the sea). The road takes a natural bend to the right and heads uphill, becoming High Ridge Road. After travelling 400m, you will sea the name Villa Coco on a wooden gate diagonally opsite Underwood Road. Ring the buzzer and welcome to Villa Coco.
