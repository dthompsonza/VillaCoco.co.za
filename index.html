Villa Coco Guesthouse

Villa Coco is a gorgeous guesthouse in Durban North and ideally situated for all of Durban's fantastic attractions. Convenient for both business and tourism, the Durban Country Club, Moses Mabhida stadium and Kings Park rugby stadium are close by, while the Durban CBD and ICC are an easy drive into the city. Restaurants and shops are just minutes away in the vibrant Broadway area and the Suncoast Casino complex, with its safe swimming beach, is only at a 3km distance.

Villa Coco is owned and run by Shawn & Michelle with that "home-away from home" feeling. The guesthouse comprises of three freestanding units which are accessed off High Ridge road and the Main House (with dining areas, reading and TV lounges and braai (BBQ) facilities) is accessible via Kelvin Place.
There is off-street parking for six vehicles.
