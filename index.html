Villa Coco Guesthouse

Villa Coco is a gorgeous guesthouse in Durban North and ideally situated for all of Durban's fantastic attractions. Convenient for both business and tourism, the Durban Country Club, Moses Mabhida stadium and Kings Park rugby stadium are close by, while the Durban CBD and ICC are an easy drive into the city. Restaurants and shops are just minutes away in the vibrant Broadway area and Suncoast Casino complex, with its safe swimming beach, is only at a 3km distance.

Villa Coco is owned and run by Shawn & Michelle with that "home away from home" feeling. The guesthouse comprises of two free-standing units which are accessed off High Ridge Road. There is off street parking for two vehicles.
