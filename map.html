Villa Coco Guesthouse

 

View Villa Coco B&B on a larger map

Note: The physical address is 43 Kelvin Place, Durban North - however, access to the guesthouse is off High Ridge Road which runs parallel to Kelvin.

Directions to Villa Coco from King Shaka International Airport:

Take N2 South (Durban Bound).
Travel for approx. 10km once on the N2 and take Umhlanga/Gateway off-ramp.
Turn left at traffic lights onto M41 and continue for approx. 3km to then get onto M4 South (running parallel to the sea).
Travel for approx. 5km and take Swapo / Durban North off-ramp. Go over M4.
Take first left after yield sign into Old Mill Way. Engen on left and soccer field on right.
Head up Old Mill Way and at second set of traffic lights, turn left into Kenneth Kaunda Road (previously know as Northway).
Go over bridge and up the hill until first set of traffic lights. Turn right into Romsey Grove.
Turn left at stop street into Gainsborough Drive, which becomes High Ridge Road.
Pass the park on your left and a few hundred meters down on your left will be the Villa Coco Guesthouse entrance (two big pot plants on either side of wooden gate with Villa Coco on the gate).

Directions to Villa Coco from N3 / Johannesburg:

Pass Pavilion Shopping Centre in Westville.
Follow N3 Durban signs which become N3 City.
Stay left after Brickfield Road sign and take Ridge Road / Berea off-ramp.
Turn left at traffic lights into North Ridge Road and head north for 2.5km.
At traffic light (T-junction) turn right into Springfield Road (M17) and travel for 2.2km downhill towards the sea.
Cross Umgeni Road.
Turn left at Durban North sign (M12) and travel for 3.4km.
Pass Moses Mabhida stadium on your left and cross over bridge (Umgeni River).
Take slip-road into Tyne Place (at Chicken Licken) which becomes High Ridge Road.
Proceed up hill for approx. 200m and you will come to Villa Coco Guesthouse entrance on your right (two big pot plants on either side of wooden gate with Villa Coco on the gate).
