Villa Coco Guesthouse

 

View Villa Coco B&B in a larger map

Note: The physical address is 43 Kelvin Place, Durban North - however, access to the guesthouse is off High Ridge Road which runs parallel to Kelvin.

Directions to Villa Coco from King Shaka International Airport

Take N2 South (Durban Bound)
Travel for approx 10km once on N2 and take Umhlanga/Gateway off-ramp.
Turn left at traffic lights onto M41 and continue with M41 for approx 3km to get onto M4 South (running parallel to the sea)
Travel for approx 5km and take Swapo/Durban North off-ramp. Go over M4
Take first left after yield sign into Old Mill Way. Engen on left and soccer field on right
Head up Old Mill Way and at second set of traffic lights turn left into Kenneth Kaunda Road (previously known as Northway)
Go over bridge and up the hill until first set of traffic lights and turn right into Romsey
Turn left at stop street into Gainsborough. Park on your left and just a few hundred metres down on your left you will come across the Villa Coco Guesthouse entrance.

Directions from N3 .... Johannesburg

Pass Pavillion Shopping centre Westville
Follow N3 Durban signs which become N3 City
Stay left after Brickfield Road sign and take Ridge Road / Berea off-ramp
Turn left at traffic light into North Ridge Road and head north for 2.5km
At traffic light (t-junction) turn right into Springfield Road (M17) and travel for 2.2km downhill towards the sea
Cross Umgeni Road
Turn left at Durban North sign (M12)
Travel for 3.4km
Pass new Moses Mabhida soccer stadium on your left
Cross over bridge (Umgeni River)
Turn left into slip-road, Tyne (at Chicken Licken) which becomes High Ridge
Proceed up hill for approx 200m and you will find Villa Coco on your right. There is a sign (Villa Coco) on the wooden gate with two big pots on either side of the driveway
Press the buzzer on the right hand pillar to gain entrance.
