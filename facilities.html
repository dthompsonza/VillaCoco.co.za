Villa Coco Guesthouse

The guesthouse comprises of three tastefully furnished cottages as well as the Main House. The cottages are separate from the Main House and consist of en-suite bath and/or shower rooms, own private deck, bar fridge, aircon and DSTV.

The cottages have their own communal bar area, ideally suited for a braai (BBQ).

The Main House has self-catering facilities, reading and TV lounges, indoor and outdoor dining areas and an outdoor lounge with spectacular ocean views.

In the grounds there is a lovely swimming pool with surrounding garden. This allows for kids and adults to enjoy themselves on the property. There is also secure off-street parking available.

Please note the address of the guesthouse is 43 Kelvin Place however there are dual entrances. One entrance is off Kelvin Place and the other from High Ridge Road.
