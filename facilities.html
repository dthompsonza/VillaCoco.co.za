Villa Coco Guesthouse

The Guesthouse consists of three tastefully furnished cottages as well as the Main House. The cottages are separate from the Main House and consist of en suite bath or shower, own private deck, bar fridge, aircon, and DSTV.

The cottages have their own bar-area, ideally suited for braaing (BBQ).

The Main four-bedroom House is accessible off Kelvin Place, and has a double lock-up garage.

In the grounds there is a lovely swimming pool with surrounding garden. This allows for kids and adults to enjoy themselves on the property. There is also secure off-street parking available.

Main House

The Main House consists of one extra large en suite bedroom, a second en suite bedroom and two bedrooms with a shared bathroom. All bedrooms are tastefully decorated in tropical style, with aircon and DSTV.
There is a TV lounge at the front of the house and dining room which opens onto a deck area overlooking the ocean. This can also be used as a braai (BBQ) area.
The Main House is ideally suited for families or groups of friends with a combination of double and single beds.
There is also a large, fully kitted out kitchen for self catering purposes.

Prices:

Rates from R300 per person sharing, ex VAT. Rates vary according to season, length of stay and room choice.

Full Continental Breakfast is inclusive (selection of cereals, fruit and yoghurts, juice, tea and coffee.

Children between the ages of 3 and 12 pay R200 per night. Children under 3 stay for free.
