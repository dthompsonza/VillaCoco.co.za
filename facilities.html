Villa Coco Guesthouse

The guesthouse comprises of two tastefully furnished cottages. The cottages are separate form the main main house and consist of ensuite bath and/or shower rooms, own private deck, air con, bar fridge and self-catering facilities if required.

The cottages have their own communal bar area, ideally suited for a braai (BBQ).

Please note the address of the guesthouse is 43 Kelvin Place with the guesthouse cottages only accessible via High Ridge Road. The entrance via Kelvin Place is for the Main House.
